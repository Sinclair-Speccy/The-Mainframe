Guardians of the School setting

Guardians of the School is based on my personal experiences in real life, therefore the setting for this project is based on an alternative universe of our universe. Most of the story takes place in Meadows Ridge, a fictional city in Australia..

There is also the "Other World", more commonly known as Ozzer Vorld. It's a place where ghosts and people with supernatural powers can go to as an escape from reality, but also a place where evil spirits who are contained for their crimes attempt to reach out into reality to escape their containment.
