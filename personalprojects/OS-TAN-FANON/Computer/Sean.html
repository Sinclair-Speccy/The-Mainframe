Character

Born in The Infinite Loop, Sean's early days were loving and nurturing by his mother, Stephanie. However, he was marked by physical and mental challenges. Despite the gentle and caring nature of his mother, Sean faced structural fragility, both in terms of physical health and mental well-being. Even though he was born to be a businessman, he felt like his purpose was taken from him by his sister because of his troubled birth. Not only was his body fragile, but he was also prone to not being able to control his fire powers and could not read a clock properly. His outfit consists of a brown vest, a tan long-sleeved shirt, a beige cape, black jeans with rips in them and black shoes.
