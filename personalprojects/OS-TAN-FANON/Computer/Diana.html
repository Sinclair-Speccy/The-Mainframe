Character

Diana is a girl with long brown hair. She usually ties her hair back with a white hippie bandana, giving her a laid-back and carefree vibe. Her outfits consist of a cream-coloured button-down shirt with brown patches, beige pants, a brown leather belt adorned with a badge sporting the rainbow Apple logo with "Apple ][" written on it and shoes that are short and black with red LEDs. Sometimes she also wears a newsboy cap.

Family and Relationships

Apple I-tan

Diana loves her mother very much and was one of the first to find out her mother had “died”, until it was discovered that she never died in the first place. A part of her hippie personality comes from her mother.

Apple III-kun

The older sister to her older brother Sean, she tried to reassure him when he failed at what his "purpose" was. Since businesses used the Apple II which is what the III was created for, he sometimes feels like she stole his purpose.

Lisa-tan

Diana doesn't think much of Lisa.

TRS-80-kun and Commodore PET-kun

Diana, TRS-80-kun, and Commodore PET-kun form a tight-knit trio reminiscent of the 1977 Trinity. However, she often snarks to them about how her parent company survived unlike theirs.

Abilities

She wields a polearm that has a chain attached to it. At the end of the chain there is a grappling hook which she uses for various tasks.
