Character

Byt-8-kun's name isn't really Byt-8, but it is better than using "Erector Set model MP-210". Plus Byt-8 is the name on the front of the case. He often wears a black and silver old-fashioned shop owner outfit as a nod to the mostly black and silver white cases for the Byt-8 computers even though some cases vary slightly in design.

Family and Relationships

It is unknown whether Byt-8-kun had any family members or relatives as the Byt-8 was provided only to the Byte Shop stores so they could get a better profit margin than could be achieved by just distributing the computers of the other computer manufacturers at the time, unless you count the Exidy Sorcerer (because the creator wanted a consumer computer that was user-friendly beyond anything currently in the marketplace), or the Cromemcos (S-100 CPU, RAM and I/O boards were made by Cromemco) but he did have various friends who were the microcomputer kits Byte Shop sold.

  • Altair 8080-tan: The Byte Shop was the first retailer of the original Apple I computer meaning that Apple I-tan would have been one of the few first people he met.
  • Apple I-tan: The Byte Shop was the first retailer of the original Apple I computer meaning that Apple I-tan would have been one of the few first people he met.
  • Exidy Sorcerer-tan: Exidy Sorcerer-tan is sort of the non-blood member to Byt-8-kun although it is unknown whether they ever met as the Byte Shop chain was sold to John Peers of Logical Machine Corporation.

Abilities

Byt-8-kun doesn't really have any abilities or weapons.
