Character

Simon wears Thunder coloured (#2A292A) corduroy pants and madras sportcoat, Cararra coloured (#E8E6DD) polo shirt, a white (#FEFFFF) tie and ash-coloured (#C8C5BB) boots. The button things on the top of the Simon are small and various coloured patches on his suit and his pants too. He also wears black rectangle glasses.

Family and Relationships

GENIAC-tan

GENIAC holds a special place and close connection with Simon. GENIAC-tan deeply admires Simon and the crucial role he plays as an educational tool to showcase the wonders of digital computing. Their bond is one of respect and appreciation for each other's unique roles in the world of technology.

Abilities

Simon possesses limited computational abilities due to his design as an educational demonstration device. With only 2-bits of memory, his practical applications are highly restricted, however, his main strength lies in his role as an iconic representation of early digital computers.

Simon excels in providing educational demonstrations to help users understand the basic concepts of computing and the inner workings of a digital computer. His ability to simplify complex concepts and engage learners makes him a valuable resource for educational purposes.
