Overview

Wanderers are characters who are not part of any faction or wish to be part of one. This could be for many reasons such as their parent faction falling to pieces or something else. Although in The Mainframe it is put under as a "faction", it isn't really a faction. This is just to split it away from the factions and to have some characters in a category if they don't belong in one.
