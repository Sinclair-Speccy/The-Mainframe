Overview

The League of the Kernel was founded from a result of more people gaining powers granted from The Kernel from increased popularity and was a way to keep track of everyone who became a follower of The Kernel. Although she is not directly controlling it, The Kernel plays a part in keeping it stable along with well known Linux distros helping her keep it stable.
