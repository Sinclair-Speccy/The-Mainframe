Overview

The Nokian Liittovaltio was one of the most well-known and somewhat wealthiest of these vintage factions until iPhone 3G-tan began to rise in dominance. It was said that the Nokian Liittovaltio was founded by Nokia SV-1300-tan in 1974 on the company's pulp mill but she spent most of her time as a loner.
