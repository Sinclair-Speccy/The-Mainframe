Character

Macintosh System 3, known as Saburo McIntosh, is one of the triplets alongside his siblings Macintosh System 2 and Macintosh System 1. Just like his siblings, Saburo shares similar eye color and hair color, emphasizing their familial connection. In terms of attire, Saburo dons a grey and black suit paired with a matching tie. His short, dark hair and glasses further enhance his polished appearance, giving him a scholarly and knowledgeable aura. Saburo McIntosh embodies traits such as intellect, keen observation, and a remarkable memory. Known for his exceptional ability to remember vast amounts of information, he serves as the repository of knowledge among the siblings.

Family and Relationships

Macintosh System 1 & 2

Out of the triplets, he tries to stay out of trouble but seems to fail most of the time.

Apple Lisa

His formal and intellectual personality is a trait he inherited from her, as she herself possesses a formal and scholarly demeanor.

XEROX Alto

The son of XEROX Alto-kun, Macintosh System 3-kun and Xerox Alto-kun have a complicated relationship. Alto is the father of System 3, but he left Lisa when the triplets were young. As a result, System 3 doesn't know much about him and hardly ever sees him. The only time System 3 really got to know his father was when Alto tried to sue Lisa for custody of the triplets In the end, Lisa won custody, and Alto was ordered to pay child support.

XENIX

Macintosh System 3-kun is aware of XENIX-tan because of Lisa-tan.

System 6

System 6 and System 3.4 share the same finder version number so they are sometimes seen as stepsiblings, despite System 3 being older than System 6 and System 6 not having any parents unlike System 3.
