Character

Macintosh System 3, known as Saburo McIntosh, is one of the triplets alongside his siblings Macintosh System 2 and Macintosh System 1. Like his sibling they all share the same hair colour and eye colour.

Saburo wears a grey (#a8a8a8) and black (#1a1917) suit paired with a matching tie and rectangular glasses. He is not much of a fighter and more of known for his exceptional ability to remember vast amounts of information that he is seen as the more formal and smarter out of his siblings... even though sometimes he fills trash bins up enough that they bulge and explode.

Unlike his siblings he seemed to be a little more friendly unlike Prima who was boisterous and Fuji who was more shy.

round 1986 he ended up really buggy for unknown reasons and the higherups had to get some doctors to tend to him, but he got better quickly. During his time System 4 and System 6 made their debuts so he helped them with their debuts.

Family and Relationships

Macintosh System 1 & 2

Out of the triplets, he tries to stay out of trouble but seems to fail most of the time.

Apple Lisa & XENIX

Although she is his mother, not much of his traits is actually from her. More of it is from XEROX Alto-kun himself. Since Lisa was friends with XENIX-tan he got to meet her too.

XEROX Alto

The son of XEROX Alto, the two of them have a complicated relationship. Alto is the father of System 3 but he left Lisa when the triplets were young. As a result System 3 doesn't know much about him and hardly ever sees him. The only time System 3 really got to know his father was when Alto tried to sue Lisa for custody of the triplets. In the end, Lisa won custody, and Alto was ordered to pay child support.

System 6

They are sometimes seen as stepsiblings, despite System 3 being older than System 6 and System 6 not having any parents unlike System 3.

References and sources

  • Wikipedia entry on Classic Mac OS with System 3.
  • His good memory is a reference to Disk Cache which would store the most used instructions to memory.
  • Saburo and Aldebaran being "related" is a reference to how System Software 3.4 was released after System 4 and just before System 6. They share the same finder version which is version 6.1. Other versions of 3.4 use version 5.X (X for another number) as their finder version). This is the System 6.0 finder version which is 6.1 as well
  • Him being known to make trash cans bulge is a reference to how in System 3.0, the Trash Can "bulged" when something was thrown away.
  • System 3.0 was the first version to come with an installer.
  • System 3.1 was shortlived as it had serious flaws impacting file storage and system stability. You could say System 3.1.1 fixed these but System 3.2 fixed over 30 bugs which most likely included these flaws. System Software 3.1.1 was only on the Guided Tour disk for the 512Ke, but I guess you can say it *did* fix some of the bugs in the older version. 3.1.1 wasn't really a standalone version unlike 3.2, hence why I am not counting it for when he got better.
