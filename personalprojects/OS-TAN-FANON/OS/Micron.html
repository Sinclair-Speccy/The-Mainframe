Character

Windows NT-kun is a tall and muscular young man with short navy hair and teal eyes. He always has a serious expression on his face, a testament to Windows NT's roots with VMS and its focus on business and server environments. NT-kun dons a well-tailored ultramarine suit, white dress shirt, and burgundy tie. Completing the look, he wears sleek, black leather dress shoes, emphasizing his commitment to excellence and precision.

NT-kun is a very serious and dedicated individual. He is always focused on his work and he is always looking for ways to improve. He is also very intelligent and he has a deep understanding of technology. He also has some shark features, like a shark fin and tail.

Family and Relationships

Windows 95

Despite their differences, the bond between Micron and Windows 95-tan is strengthened by their mutual admiration for stability and security. The strong and unyielding nature of NT-kun resonates with Rachel's love for his unyielding foundation, recognizing his reputation as a staunch and stable operating system, similar to Windows 95-tan's own attributes.

Rachel's passion for the '90s, an era that coincides effortlessly with NT-kun's flexibility across multiple platforms despite his composite composition of human, alien, and shark traits. In discussions on the expanding world of technology, they share views on how to retain simplicity and dependability in an ever-changing realm. Despite their roots being from separate lineages, their outstanding collaboration and coordination are evidence of their combined efforts.

Abilities

Micron's abilities are a reflection of Windows NT's robust and stable nature. Just as Windows NT boasted exceptional stability and reliability, Micron possesses the innate ability to withstand digital disruptions and maintain his composure even in the face of unexpected glitches or crashes. This resilience ensures that he can continue functioning effectively within The Mainframe, regardless of the challenges he encounters.
