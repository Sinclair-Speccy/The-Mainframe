Character

Tylan has short brown hair, yellow eyes, and wears a futuristic jumpsuit in navy blue with accents of yellow, light blue, and white. His outfit is stylish and functional, reflecting the tile-based interface of Windows 8 and 8.1. He also wears a helmet with a light blue visor, gloves, and boots.

He's an explorer, adaptable, and driven by a passion for modernization and progress, much like the OS's attempt to blend touchscreen and desktop experiences. Tylan can be unpredictable at times, reflecting Windows 8's dual-interface approach, but he values compatibility and seeks common ground with others. Initially, Tylan embraced a touchscreen-centric approach, aiming to modernize user interactions in The Mainframe; however, this shift proved jarring for users familiar with traditional desktops, leading to frustration. Tylan responded by working to bridge the touchscreen-desktop gap, but his reputation was already damaged. Despite updates and fixes, the initial backlash left a lasting mark, making it a challenging journey of reinvention for him.
