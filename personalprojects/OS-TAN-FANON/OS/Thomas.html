Character

Born from the collaboration of Windows 1.0-tan and MS-DOS-kun, Windows 2.X-kun, or Thomas, stands as an embodiment of innovation and unity within The Mainframe. He seamlessly melds the qualities of his parents, inheriting his mother's reliability and his father's innovation. He wears an Electric Blue button-down shirt with Screamin' Green jeans and white sneakers.

Thomas boasts not one, but two distinctive personas: the 8086 real mode and his empowered alter ego, Windows/386. The former thrives on efficient multitasking, powered by the revolutionary concept of true overlapping windows. His alter-ego outfit consists of a form-fitting black bodysuit, a bold neon Screamin' Green leather jacket. Ultramarine blue cargo pants and black combat boots offer both style and practicality. Electric blue sunglasses add a flair to the cyberpunk-inspired look.

His Windows/386 alter-ego, preceding Windows 2.0's release, empowered Thomas to navigate The Mainframe while being able to steer clear of threats. Windows/386 elevates Thomas's multitasking prowess, allowing multiple MS-DOS programs to run concurrently in the "virtual 8086" CPU mode. This achievement shatters the barriers of suspending background applications, enabling seamless orchestration of tasks.

A crowning feature is its EMS emulation, leveraging the i386's memory management to extend memory beyond 640k. Despite challenges, Thomas's strategic ingenuity maximizes resource utilization, ensuring optimal performance.

Thomas's groundbreaking contribution lies in his introduction of true overlapping windows, gradually securing The Windows Order's dominion within The Mainframe.

Family and Relationships

MS-DOS

Thomas deeply appreciates the constant support and guidance he's received from MS-DOS-kun as he's grown into Windows 2.X-kun. He knows he owes a big part of his progress to the strong base MS-DOS-kun provided. It's like MS-DOS-kun's experience and groundwork had given Thomas the boost he needed, helping him thrive and become a key player in the world of computing.

Windows 1.X

Windows 2.X-kun holds a cherished place in Windows 1.0-tan's heart. She takes pride in his growth and the way he has inherited her reliability and practicality. His introduction of true overlapping windows, a hallmark of his era, brought a harmonious sense of multitasking to their digital realm. This contribution to their shared family legacy makes Windows 1.0-tan deeply grateful and proud. The way Windows 2.X-kun has blossomed reflects not only his own evolution but also the nurturing environment fostered by his caring mother, Windows 1.0-tan.

Abilities

  • Enhanced Memory Management: Thomas showcases exceptional memory skills, effortlessly recalling intricate details and information.
  • VGA Graphics: Leveraging advancements in graphics technology, Thomas deftly manipulates visual elements with creativity and innovation.
