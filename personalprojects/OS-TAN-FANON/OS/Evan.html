Character

Windows 3.X-kun is a war veteran who found a new purpose after his service. He now runs a bustling hotdog stand, offering delicious hotdogs at the troupe's carnivals. He wears a dark blue tracksuit that reflects his active lifestyle and practicality. The tracksuit provides comfort and freedom of movement, allowing him to efficiently serve hotdogs to customers with a dash of agility.

He tends to stand up for the people he cares about most. He also likes to play Solitaire.

Windows 3.0 MME equipped Windows 3.X-kun with the necessary tools to explore and harness the power of multimedia. He learned to utilize the Multimedia Control Interface (MCI), which served as a standardized framework for controlling multimedia devices and applications. This allowed him to seamlessly interact with various multimedia peripherals and create captivating audiovisual experiences. Windows 3.0 with Multimedia Extensions allowed him to make his hotdog stand well, stand out more to people.

Family and Relationships

MS-DOS

Windows 3.X-kun has a close relationship with MS-DOS-kun. MS-DOS-kun is the foundation on which Windows 3.X-kun is built, and the two of them work together. MS-DOS-kun is a bit old-fashioned, but he is reliable and dependable. Windows 3.X-kun respects MS-DOS-kun for his experience and knowledge, and he is grateful for MS-DOS-kun.

Windows 95

While he is proud of her accomplishments, he sometimes feels a sense of unease and insecurity, fearing that Windows 95-tan might overshadow him as she is always trying to show off her new features. However, deep down, Windows 3.X-kun still loves and supports Windows 95-tan, knowing that they are part of the same Windows family and have their respective roles to play.

Windows NT

Despite their differences, Windows 3.X-kun and Windows NT-kun share a mutual respect for each other's contributions to the Windows ecosystem. Windows 3.X-kun appreciates Windows NT-kun's ability to seamlessly handle different hardware platforms and software environments. He admires Windows NT-kun's broad software compatibility, security features like access control lists, and support for advanced networking protocols.

Abilities

Due to his background as an operating system with a user-friendly interface, Windows 3.X-kun has a natural talent for connecting with people.
