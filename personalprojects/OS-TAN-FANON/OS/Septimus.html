Character

Septimus is of average height with neatly groomed blonde hair and orange eyes. He wears a fancy outfit largely in shades of blue, featuring intricate patterns and subtle metallic accents. Additionally, he dons a stylish flowing cape. He wields a sleek and polished silver katana with a little blue flag on the hilt.

Septimus has a calm and composed demeanor and is known for his exceptional attention to detail and his adherence to protocols and standards. He possesses an organized and analytical mindset, enabling him to tackle complex challenges with ease. Despite his formal nature, he is approachable and accommodating. Compared to other Windows characters, Septimus is often found in the Security Center monitoring security and maintenance for the Windows faction. He is always on the lookout for potential threats and is quick to take action to mitigate them.

Family and Relationships

Windows Vista

Septimus's relationship with Windows Vista-tan can be seen as a journey of growth and improvement. He carefully observed and analyzed the strengths and weaknesses of his predecessor, striving to overcome the challenges faced by Windows Vista-tan. Septimus's goal was to provide a more stable, efficient, and user-friendly operating system. He learned from the mistakes made by his predecessor and focused on delivering a more refined and reliable experience. Septimus acknowledges the efforts made by Windows Vista-tan in pushing the boundaries of visual aesthetics and introducing new features. He appreciates the ambition and creativity displayed by Windows Vista-tan, recognizing her role. Despite him being encouraging to her, Vista has resentment over feeling like she is being overshadowed by her younger brother.

Windows XP

Septimus, with his composed nature and strict adherence to protocols and standards, finds himself at odds with Felix's more carefree and sometimes clumsy personality. Felix, known for his quirks and the reputation of being the "king" of the Windows faction, takes delight in teasing Septimus whenever they cross paths. The contrast between their approaches to computing and problem-solving leads to amusing interactions, with Felix finding amusement in playfully annoying Septimus.

Abilities

While he has a katana, he can also turn slightly transparent with Windows Aero.
