Character

Marvin wears a ripped shirt that is various shades of blue, has tattoos on his upper arms, long wavy brown hair, a necklace with a seashell, grey shorts and a brown bracelet. He’s got a knack for surfing, is energetic, very sociable, friendly, and helpful. He also has a good sense of direction, a love for reading and learning new things and adventurous which all narrows down to features of Mavericks. Despite all this, he tries to be generous and altruistic, as he likes to share his skills with others without expecting anything in return. He also speaks surfer slang and has a surfboard. Marvin is sometimes seen as "abandonware" to humans but that doesn't stop him from trying to get followers years after.
