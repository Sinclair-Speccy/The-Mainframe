Character

Felix is a cheerful and nostalgic character. He has short brown hair and subtle facial hair. Felix's green eyes are from the iconic Windows XP Bliss wallpaper. Felix is often seen in a grey and light blue jumper paired with a dark blue shirt and dark green jeans.

Felix maintains a laid-back and friendly demeanor. He enjoys sharing funny Flash parodies and stories of the early internet days. Felix is known for his ability to evoke nostalgia and bring back the joy of the early 2000s online culture. He is also Charli's biggest fanboy, even when he was a beta by copying her style.

Felix's favorite pastime is watching old Flash animations, exploring vintage websites, and celebrating the creativity of the early internet. He embraces the charm of the past while staying connected to the digital world.

His fondness for the early days of the internet and platforms like YouTube, reliability, adaptability, and compatibility resonated with humans, evoking nostalgia for simpler digital times. Much like Windows XP's longevity, Felix's enduring presence in The Mainframe secured his status as one of the well-known OS-kuns in The Mainframe, making him an iconic figure.

Family and Relationships

Windows 2000

Felix respects William for his reputation as a stable and reliable operating system, particularly for business use. Although their personalities differ significantly, William appreciates Felix's enthusiasm for the early Internet era.

Abilities

While he may appear as a charming and nostalgic character, he also has an unexpected secret: the ability to transform into a magical boy wielding a mighty Warhammer that can shoot rays of light.
