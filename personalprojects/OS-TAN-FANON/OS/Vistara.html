Character

Vistara is a calm and methodical magical girl. Her personality reflects the nature of Windows Vista, showcasing both its strengths and weaknesses. Much like the operating system, she is a complex character with a mix of attributes. Demonstrating an unwavering dedication to her duties and a commitment to maintaining harmony, Vistara approaches challenges with a measured and thoughtful approach.

However, Vistara is not without her flaws, akin to Vista's reputation for being buggy upon release. At times, she can be overly cautious, and hesitant to take risks or embrace change. She sometimes struggles with adaptability and occasionally finds herself stuck in traditional ways of thinking. Yet she is continually trying to learn so she can overcome her bad reputation with UAC and much more.

She has long, flowing brown hair and green eyes. Vistara's outfit is a bodysuit that features blues and greens mirroring the Vista wallpaper. Translucent, glassy panels adorn her boots and gloves, paying homage to Vista's signature look. Her tiara which has a holographic Windows Vista logo, symbolizes her connection to the OS.

Family and Relationships

Windows XP

Vistara is drawn to Felix's appreciation for the early days of the internet and digital culture, but she still feels undermined by him.

Windows 7

Septimus appreciates Vistara's commitment to trying to save the world although he feels slightly bad for overcoming the challenges faced by her.

Abilities

Vistara's primary weapon is the "Vista Staff." It's a staff with a crystal orb at the tip that emits a soothing, supernatural light. It is mostly used for summoning shields and banishing malware.
