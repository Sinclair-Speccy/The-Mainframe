Character

Miles embodies the spirit of the late '90s computing era in The Mainframe, welcoming users with a cheerful and vibrant personality just like Windows 95-tan. He wears a light blue windbreaker jacket adorned with patterns and a large Windows logo on the back, a t-shirt featuring a pattern of floppy disk icons, and loose-fitting jeans with patches of various computer-related icons. The icons include the mouse cursor, floppy disk, and CD-ROM drive.

His chunky sneakers resemble the footwear popular in the late '90s and feature a mix of light blue and grey shades. Around his neck, Miles proudly wears a lanyard with a USB drive, symbolizing the growing importance of USB technology during that era.

He also has spiky, slightly disheveled hair.

Miles's vibrant appearance mirrors his lively personality. He is a friendly and approachable tour guide within The Mainframe, known for his genuine enthusiasm and love for all things '90s, including music, fashion, and technology. He enjoys making references to '90s pop culture, video games, and technology.

Family and Relationships

Windows 95

While both Miles and Rachel hail from the vibrant '90s computing era, they have a lot in common. They both share the same basic design principles, and they both use the same underlying operating system technology.

However, there are also some key differences between Windows 98-kun and Windows 95-tan. Windows 98-kun is a more advanced operating system and has a number of new abilities that Windows 95-tan does not have. For example, Windows 98-kun has better support for USB devices and the internet.

Abilities

Miles possesses a unique ability tied to the integration of Internet Explorer within Windows 98, but it's both a blessing and a curse for him.

On the one hand, it allows him to do things that would be impossible for a normal operating system at the time of the '90s if they did not have a web browser. He can search the internet for information, download files, and much more. This makes him a very powerful tool.

On the other hand, his ability also makes him vulnerable. If someone were to make him surrender, they could control him and use him to do their bidding. This is a serious threat, and Miles must be careful to protect himself.
