Character

William is a dedicated and industrious individual. He is recognized in The Windows Order for his exceptional intelligence and capabilities, and he takes immense pride in his work. William is driven by a profound commitment to excellence, striving for perfection in every task he undertakes.

One notable aspect of William's character is his unyielding perfectionism. He possesses an innate need for precision and exactness in all aspects of his work and life. This perfectionist streak occasionally makes him demanding, as he has an unwavering dedication to achieving the highest standards of quality. He refuses to settle for mediocrity and doesn't hesitate to push both himself and others to reach their full potential. His meticulous attention to detail ensures that every project he tackles is executed flawlessly.

William's attire is a reflection of his professional and business-oriented demeanor. He typically dons a navy blue blazer, a white or light blue button-down shirt, khaki or navy chinos and a pair of brown or black leather shoes.

Family and Relationships

Windows NT-kun

They both value excellence and professionalism. Micron's impeccable attire reflects his dedication to precision, aligning with William's perfectionism. Their mutual respect is rooted in their shared pursuit of excellence. William and Micron are highly intelligent and possess deep technological understanding.
