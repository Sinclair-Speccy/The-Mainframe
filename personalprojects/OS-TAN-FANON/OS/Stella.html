Character

Stella is a dark-skinned and elegant character, and formal besides OS X Lion-tan. Her outfit consists of a long, flowing dress that is primarily blue and light blue which drapes down to her feet and conceals her tail, and features a high collar along with a red rascot tie of sorts that has a light blue shiny Apple logo above it. She has long blue hair that reaches further than her hips, orange cat eyes and both a panther tail and ears where human ears would usually be. Stella also wears long dark blue gloves, long dark blue tights and boots that are made up of black, dark blue and white. A metal worker at heart and a linguist, Stella is known as being one of of the more formal out of the OS X cats in The Infinite Loop. Stella is very skilled and meticulous in her work as a metal worker. She can craft, repair, and modify various metal objects and devices, such as weapons, armor, tools, and jewelry. Although formal, she can be suggestive too but you have to get to know her well enough to experience this. To outsiders, what she does is secretive as she is not open about her life to strangers unless she gets to know them well.

Family and Relationships

Other OSX Cats

Most of the OS X cats are closely connected with each other, however Stella is more close to Leopard/Snow Leopard-tan and Jaguar-tan, noting that black panthers are the melanistic colour variant of the leopard and the jaguar.

Mavericks-kun

Stella is curious about OS X Mavericks-kun who is not named after a big cat, but after a surfing location in California. She tends to tease him like Mojave teases Mavericks too.
