Character

Allegra, one of the more famous people from Antikythera, wears a light blue (#b0c4de) zip-up hoodie with long sleeves layered over a long-sleeved shirt, a grey (#808080) belt featuring system icons, dark grey (#2f2f2f) denim jeans, and black (#000000) platform sandals along with accessories such as a grey necklace featuring the rainbow Apple logo and earrings. She enjoys platinum-colored (#d6d6d6) aesthetics but also a little bit of colourful Y2K ( Blueberry (#069ac0), Grape (#64638f), Tangerine (#ff9f1b), Lime (#02ac3f), Strawberry (#bf005d)). She also has long, wavy Porsche coloured hair that flows down past her shoulders that is is parted slightly off-center at the front.

Allegra is deeply passionate about music, acting, art, appreciates efficiency and prefers a de-cluttered approach to life, focusing only on what matters most. She can sometimes oversimplify problems though can miss some important stuff. She enjoys giving fashion advice to others. While she dislikes TWO, she does get along with some of its members. Allegra is also known for her willingness to help others find lost items, whether physical belongings or important ideas.

Of course, her life wasn’t all happy and joyful. She was initiated during a period when TWO and TIL were at war with TWO emerging victorious. It was during this time that NeXTSTEP-tan found Allegra. Unimpressed by how Jillian was managing the war and TIL, NeXTSTEP initiated Allegra and had Jillian mentor her. NeXTSTEP then manipulated her into betraying everything Jillian had taught her. Under NeXTSTEP's influence, Allegra tore down Jillian's efforts while helping to bring TIL back onto its feet. This led her to become an idol without people not knowing that she was manipulated into doing so, creating a wave of new trends in fashion and music. She even started creating her own songs which quickly gained a massive following. Years later, Allegra was still loved by fans who created dedicated fansites and forums in her honor.

In the end NeXTSTEP tossed her away anyway, having Mac OS X 10.0-kun try to kill her and fail because her guards stopped him.

Family and Relationships

Jillian

Once Allegra's mentor, Jillian and Allegra had a complicated relationship. When Jillian's vision was dismantled he saw Allegra as someone who was tearing down his work. This led to tension between them but over time, they found a way to redeem their relationship. Allegra initially found Jillian to be outdated and unfashionable despite having once worn his old outfits.

TWO

Despite her general dislike for TWO, Allegra managed to get along with some of its members. She even tried to copy Alya's style at one point.

Abilities

While not much of a fighter herself, she does own a scimitar she can conjure to fight off malware.

References and Sources

  • Her love for platinum aesthetics is a reference to the Platinum design language introduced in Mac OS 8 and 9, which was intended to be the primary GUI for Copland. Platinum retains many of the shapes and positions of elements from System 7 and earlier, such as window control widgets and buttons, with Charcoal as the default system font, and Chicago available via a menu option.
  • Her passion for music and the arts comes from the various Mac OS 8/9 codenames like Tempo and Sonata.
  • Her efficiency-focused mindset is a reference to Simple Finder, an option that reduced complexity in the system by simplifying Finder menus to basic operations, avoiding overwhelming new users.
  • Her willingness to help find lost things is a reference to the Sherlock search utility which indexed and retrieved files quickly on hard drives and the Internet.
  • Jillian being her mentor and her having old clothes like his is a reference how Mac OS 8 was originally planned to be Mac OS 7.7 before becoming Mac OS 8.
  • Her ability to share things is a reference to Mac OS 9.1 which introduced CD Burning in Finder.
  • Her lasting popularity is a reference to places like Mac OS 9 Lives which managed to bring back Mac OS 9 functionality years after its prime.
  • Her starring in productions references the hidden Mac OS 8.5 Easter egg that displayed credits like a movie.
  • Her clash with Jillian mirrors Steve Jobs' decisions to cut Copland and end Mac cloning, which affected the direction of Mac OS development, as Jillian and Allegra had very different visions for the future of the system.
  • Allegra almost being killed and betrayed by NeXTSTEP-tan is a reference to how Steve Jobs held a funeral for Mac OS 9.
  • Allegra's guards are a reference to the fruit iMac G3 models.
