Character

Longhorn is a tall and imposing woman with captivating dark skin, piercing orange eyes, and a pair of elegantly curved horns on her head. Her attire consists of a sleek suit of shiny metal armor featuring a captivating blend of shades, including blue, orange, yellow, and white, reflecting the Windows Longhorn interface. She bears scars on her body symbolizing the challenges faced during the development of Windows Longhorn.

As a prototype for the next generation of The Windows Order's leaders, Longhorn was designed to push the boundaries of technological innovation and elevate the Windows experience to new heights. However, the development of Windows Longhorn faced numerous obstacles, leading to its eventual cancellation and Longhorn defecting from The Windows Order.

Family and Relationships

Windows Vista

Windows Vista, the successor of Windows Longhorn, holds a complex relationship with Longhorn. Initially, Windows Vista was envisioned to carry forward the legacy and innovations of Windows Longhorn. However, due to the challenges faced during the development of Longhorn, she took a different approach and had to make significant changes. This deviation caused some tension between Longhorn and Windows Vista. Though their paths diverged, Longhorn respects Windows Vista for the progress it made and appreciates the effort put into refining and optimizing the features they had envisioned together. Despite the differences, Windows Vista acknowledges Longhorn's pioneering spirit and the groundwork she laid for future Windows versions.

Windows XP

Windows XP and Longhorn share a unique mentor-protégé relationship. As a veteran member of The Windows Order, Windows XP served as a guide and confidant to Longhorn during her early days. Windows XP imparted valuable knowledge, wisdom, and leadership qualities to Longhorn, who admired and respected the experienced OS-tan deeply.

Windows 7

Windows 7, the direct successor of Windows Vista, also holds a significant connection to Longhorn. Like Vista, Windows 7 benefited from the groundwork laid by Longhorn, inheriting some of her core concepts and improvements. He recognizes Longhorn's potential and values the contributions she made to the evolution of The Windows Order. Windows 7 often engages in philosophical discussions with Longhorn, reflecting on the challenges faced during Longhorn's development and the impact of her eventual cancellation. These conversations inspire Windows 7 to make the most of her time as a prominent OS-tan and to forge her own path of progress.

Abilities

Longhorn wields a Double-Bladed Scythe. With her scythe, she can swiftly eliminate threats she encountered while being a wanderer.
