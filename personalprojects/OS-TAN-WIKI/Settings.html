Settings and Lores

Okay okay... depending on who you ask they may say there is a setting and lore for OS-tans, and no for OS-tans having a setting and lore.

But consistent details? To be fair I wouldn't call that canon either but rather the consistent details are what most people use which are things like the characters being sisters to one another.
