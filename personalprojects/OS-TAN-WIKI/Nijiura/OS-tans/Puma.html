Character

Puma-tan resembles a middle or high school student and is depicted as shy and insecure. Art suggests her not confident about swimming or might prefer more relaxing things like floating.

Appearance

Puma-tan has blue hair and green eyes. She wears a sleeveless, white-collared black blouse with a green ribbon bow and blue X's, along with fluffy socks and penny loafers.

She is depicted as resembling a middle or high school student.

Notes
