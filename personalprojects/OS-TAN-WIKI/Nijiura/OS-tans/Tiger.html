Character

Tiger-tan is a playful and spirited Mac-tan and is known for her energetic personality. Tiger-tan is depicted as helpful towards the older OS X cats, such as Cheetah and Puma, as shown in various illustrations. She may have an affinity for activities like baseball, as suggested by one artwork... though this is most likely a reference to Apple snarking at Microsoft's Longhorn.

Appearance

Tiger-tan wears a hybrid black business suit and swimsuit, adorned with a black X-shaped ribbon in her hair. She accessorizes with black arm-length gloves and full-sized leggings, often seen with a red tie featuring a white X.

Notes

  • Like most of the other Mac OS X-tans, there is a lack of information on her.
