Character

Leopard-tan has many designs, the main one from +Nijiura+ by Sato.

Appearance

Leopard-tan is characterised by her pink hair and leopard features. She wears a teal, black, and white dress, adorned with a brooch featuring the Time Machine logo.

Notes

  • Like most of the other Mac OS X-tans, there is a lack of information on her.
  • Leopard-tan has various designs across different sources, but Sato's design from +Nijiura+ is the most recognized.
