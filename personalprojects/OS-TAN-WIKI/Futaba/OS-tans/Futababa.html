Description

Futababa is the personification of Windows 2.1. She was discovered on a Japanese imageboard that requires a Japanese VPN to access. Her name in Japanese, ふた婆 (Futababa), translates to "Two grandma."

She is often depicted with 1.0-tan and 3.1-tan in various fan artworks... more notably NSFW fanworks.

Appearance

Futababa is typically seen using a cane. Her attire is usually a white, sleeveless top with the collar and other parts having blue accents. The number “2.1” is prominently displayed on the left side of the chest area along with shorts that are white with ruffled edges and have blue accents that match the top. She has a cane and large, decorative bow with white, blue and brown.

Associations

  • Seen with 1.0-tan and 3.1-tan in various fan artworks.

Notes and Trivia

  • Information on Futababa is limited due to her rarity and the difficulty of accessing certain Japanese imageboards where she is featured, so most is purely done on art with her from these image sites.
  • Like 3.1-tan, the Futaba★Secondary Backstage wiki lists her as under Windows 2.1 despite her not having a written entry there but instead under the image index at the Futaba wiki.
  • Futababa is considered one of more rarer Futaba characters. C-Chan's version is more popular due to how lesser known Futababa is.
  • While she is listed as 2.1, one of the image board she was found on had her under as Windows 2.0, though her outfit often has 2.1 so it is safe to assume she is 2.1 only.
