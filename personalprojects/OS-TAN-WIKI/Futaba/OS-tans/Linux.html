Description

Linux-tan is the personified character of Linux, a Unix-compatible open-source OS.

When she first appeared, she overwhelmed other OSes with her incredible abilities, but now she has faded into obscurity and is rarely seen.

She most likely debuted around the beginning of 2004 as the OS-tan fanbook shows the monster penguin design along with Lindows (Linspire), while the Futaba★Rainbow Board wiki has February 2004.

Appearance

She was originally depicted as a young girl with light skin, long hime-cut blueish or purple hair, red or pinkish eyes, a loose, sleeveless blue dress with gold accents, flipper-shaped shoes, a spear with multiple ribbons attached near the spearhead, and a large helmet with a penguin bill-like visor, gnu horns, and a row of gear teeth running down the middle.

This design often incorporated logos from Linux-related projects, such as the KDE "K" stamped on her helmet, the GNOME footprint logo printed on her dress, and the names of different Linux bootloaders emblazoned on the spear's ribbons.

Over time, this began to change as this design became a large bearded penguin with horns (a reference to the GNU GPL), a row of gear-teeth running down the top of his head (most likely alluding to the gear-based KDE logo), and a spear. Sadly, this design is nsfw depending on the artist.

Associations
