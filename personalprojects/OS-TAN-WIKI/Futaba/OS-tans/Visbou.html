Character

Visbou is an OS-tan that personifies Windows Vista, known for her ninja-like design among various Vista-tan variations. Visbou is portrayed as the youngest of the Vista siblings, naive, and innocent for her age. She often wears a ninja outfit inspired by the Vista logo resembling a shuriken. Unlike her older sister Vistake, she does not display negative traits.

Appearance

Visbou is typically depicted with short grey hair and brown hairclips resembling screws or Shiitake mushrooms. She wears a navy-colored ninja suit with open-sided hakama, revealing part of her legs. A signature item is her long red scarf, often reaching down to her knees.

Associations

  • Childhood friend of Reobo

    • Notes and Trivia

  • Her name would be written as "Visbou" in English while in Japanese it is written as "Bisubou". The "V" sound is often approximated with a "B" sound in Japanese, which is why you see ビ (bi) used in their names, so most other English based wikis translate only the "bi" part to "vi". Originally, Japanese had no ヴ character so they used variations of ビ (bi). You can still pronounce it as "Vi", but other Japanese wikis tend to use "Bi" instead.
