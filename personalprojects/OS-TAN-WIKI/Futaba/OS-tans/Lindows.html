Description

Lindows-tan, also known as Linspire-tan, is the personification of Lindows, a Linux distribution also known as Linspire.

Not much is known about her character as most Japanese wikis have not really documented her and have to rely on artworks with her.

Appearance

Lindows-tan is depicted as a girl with blue and green hair wearing a white outfit. Her original design features long, flowing hair, a large white shirt with the Linspire logo, white tights, and blue shoes.

Another version, more representative of Linspire, shows her with twin buns decorated with the CNR icon, hook-shaped pigtails, and a Chinese dress with gold trim and the Linspire logo. This pigtailed version is considered her current self, while the long-haired design represents her past self from when Linspire was known as Lindows. However, the original design is sometimes also used to represent Linspire-tan.

According to the Trouble Windows OS-tan fanbook she made her debut around the beginning of 2004 but was seen with the Linux monster character and not the Linux-tan design.

Associations

  • Interacts with Inu-T or Me-tan in some art.
  • Often shown along with Linux-tan and less of the the Linux penguin monster.

Notes and Trivia

  • Represents both Lindows and Linspire.
  • One Futaba post refers to her as "りんすぱいあ殿" or "Rinspia-dono".
