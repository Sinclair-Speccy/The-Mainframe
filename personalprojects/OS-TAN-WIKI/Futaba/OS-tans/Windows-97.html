Description

Windows 97-tan, sometimes known as Nana-chan, represents Windows 97, an unreleased version of Windows.

According to older Futaba Channel threads a character getting a storage facility is proof of popularity and she has one of her own.

She has even been suggested as the character for Windows 7 in certain translations:

English:

“Hmmm. I'm sorry to say that now the rivalry is boiling over.

But they are already crazy about Nana-chan!

It's the same as if they decided on Nana as the personified character of Windows 7! Great victory!

Kya~! Nana-chan is too cute, getting drunk on her own without knowing that she's been under the sun for three days!”

Appearance

Nana-chan's design has has brown hair styled in buns with braids with tassels, red a traditional Chinese-style dress shirt which features a Windows logo brooch on the collar. Underneath, she is dressed in a school swimsuit paired with shorts. The Kanji for '97' is displayed on her forehead.
