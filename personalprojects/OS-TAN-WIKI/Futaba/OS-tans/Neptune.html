Description

Neptune-tan is a OS-tan that represents Windows Neptune, one of the cancelled Windows versions.

Sometimes, she is shown with large weapons and is often depicted with characters like Windows 97, Odyssey, Moseko, and two of the Longhorn designs, suggesting they are her friends.

There have been some artworks where she appears without her visor.

Appearance

Neptune-tan ears a black sleeveless qipao with blue accents, and large red bracelets. Has a red talisman around her collar, and has mechanical headpieces. She also has long green hair in pigtails, and is almost always seen wearing a red visor.

Associations

  • Often shown with other characters like Windows 97, Odyssey, Moseko, and several Longhorn designs.

Notes and Trivia

  • Drawn by one artist at Futaba named "イオンのバイド" on Pixiv or "Ion no Baido", though it can be assumed they did not make her concept because the art styles for the sketch for Neptune's first design and their art is very different.
