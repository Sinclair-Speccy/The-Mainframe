Description

Odyssey-tan is one of the rarer characters from Futaba and is hardly ever drawn.

It can be presumed that characters like Windows 97, the two Longhorn designs, Moseko, and Neptune are her friends because artworks show her with them more than once.

Appearance

Wears a light blue dress with puffy sleeves, black and white accents, and wears a white ascot. She also has a pair of floating gold wings, and has a gold halo above her head depending on the artist

Associations

  • Often shown with other characters like Windows 97, the two Longhorn designs, Moseko, and Neptune.

Notes and Trivia

  • Represents Windows Odyssey, a cancelled Windows version.
