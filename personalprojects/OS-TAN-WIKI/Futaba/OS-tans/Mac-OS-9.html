Character

Mac OS 9-tan, often called Sonata-sensei, is the personification of Mac OS 9. She is one of the most well-known and beloved Mac OS-tans. Sonata is cheerful, modest, and responsible, often acting as a caretaker for the other Mac characters. She enjoys painting and has good relations with Windows OS-tans like ME-tan and XP-tan. Compared to Mac OS-tan, she is calm, easygoing, and dependable. Sonata acts as an older sister figure to the OSX-kuns and maintains the Mac household gently and motherly. Despite her calm demeanor and dislike of conflict, she was involved in the OS Wars, where she reportedly used apple bombs or firearms.

Appearance

She has blonde hair, varying from short and straight to longer and wavy and a white dress with the Mac face logo on the skirt, featuring a slit up the front.

She also has variants:

  • Mac OS 9.1-tan: Sometimes depicted with a 9.1 hair clip, or as a separate character with short red hair and a monocle.
  • Mac OS 9.2-tan: Rarely depicted, represented as a blonde-haired blue-eyed schoolgirl with long hair and apple-shaped hair clips.

    • According to the Trouble Windows OS-tan Fan Book around the same time as Saseko's 2nd design and 95-tan's debut, Mac anthropomorphs, a rival to the Windows OS anthropomorphs, began to emerge.

Notes and Trivia

  • Friends with ME-tan, XP-tan, Toshiaki-san.
  • OSX-kuns looks up to her and are particularly fond of her.
