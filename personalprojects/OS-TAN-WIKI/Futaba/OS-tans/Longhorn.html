Description

There are various Longhorn designs, and these seem to be the ones mostly drawn. No wiki really documents infornmation about then either so this is based on personal research.

It can be presumed that characters like Windows 97, Odyssey, Moseko, and Neptune are their friends because artworks show her with them more than once.

The shorter-looking Longhorn is said to be the taller one's sister.

Outside of these design there have been other oncepts for Longhorn designs such as this one seen in a comic called OS Zodiac, this one with wings and pointy elf ears, this one with more spiral-like horns, this one which can be found in a wallpaper with a slightly altered design, this one which is seen in an artwork with characters like the older XP-tan design and this one.

Appearance

The pink haired Longhorn design is a sleeveless, form-fitting crop top in a light blue or teal color with white accents around the edges. The top is cut high to reveal her midriff, matching light blue or teal mini-skirt with white accents. The skirt is also tight and short, shoes that match the color scheme of the outfit featuring blue or teal and white tones and two large, black, pointed horns protruding from her head.

The younger-looking yellow haired Longhorn has a pink sleeveless dress with darker pink coloured tie with the Windows logo in a diamond shape. She is also wearing black thigh-high stockings and light pink boots with pink accents that match the dress, mechanical looking elf ears attached to a headband and a small horn coming from her forehead.
