Character

MS-DOS-tan is presented as a shy girl with ponytails. She is a very minor character, even mentioned as such on her creator's site. Art depicting her is very rare. One of the few fan artworks of her shows her interacting with DOS/V. She carries around a keyboard, and is usually peeking around corners.

Appearance

MS-DOS-tan has hair in pigtails decorated with "DOS"-shaped hair ties; her hair ties are usually blue, but are sometimes depicted in the colors of the MS DOS prompt logo as shown in Windows. She wears round glasses, a white button-down blouse with a light blue skirt and suspenders.

Associations

  • Interacts with DOS/V in some artworks that exist.

    • Notes and Trivia

  • Information about MS-DOS-tan is limited, and most of it is based on scarce and rare artworks.
  • Like Trend Micro Buster-kun (also known as Virus Buster-kun), they are one of the few characters from Futaba who are rarely drawn in contray to Norton and XP Pro-tan despite being made by the same creator.
