Character

Kapone is the OS-tan that personifies KanjiTalk 7.5. She is known as a stern, trigger-happy figure with a fondness for smoking and relaxing on tatami mats. Despite her tough demeanor, she is seen as a big sister to other Mac-tans and has a soft spot for the younger OSX-kuns, whom she raised with a strict but caring attitude. She participated in the OS Wars and carries a scar from her battles. Mac OS 9-tan is her duaghter.

Design

Kapone is depicted as a blue-eyed, blonde-haired woman. She wears a light blue dress, a white top, and taskbar-style armbands. She bears a strong resemblance to a long-haired Mac OS 9-tan, but with a more serious and disgruntled personality.

Notes

  • Known for using firearms like her Thompson submachine gun during the OS Wars.
  • Has a faint scar on her face that emerges when she's agitated.
  • Often depicted relaxing on tatami mats and smoking.
  • Seen as a big sister and respected elder among the Mac family.
