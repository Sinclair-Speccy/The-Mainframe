Description

Shinzō personifies Windows Vista and is one of the lesser-known OS-kuns. He is the second of the Vista siblings, being the younger brother of Bisukei and the elder brother of Vistake and Visbou. Shinzō is characterized as a former host and a talented painter who is very protective of his sister Vistake.

Appearance

Shinzō is depicted as a man dressed in a black and red suit. He is sometimes shown with a cigarette or a baseball bat in art, contrasting with Vistake's scythe.

Notes

  • Lives far from the Windows Family and doesn't visit often.
  • Occasionally comes home to see Vistake's sketchbook or go to karaoke with Bisukei and other celebrities.
  • Very protective of his sister Vistake.
