Description

Ōjiji is an OS-kun that personifies Windows 3.1 to 3.11. He maintains a serious demeanor and enjoys traditional Japanese activities such as tea and rice crackers. Unlike his sister Ōbaba, he is accompanied by a DOS cat girl rather than DOS Kitty. Ōjiji is portrayed as the elder brother of Ōbaba and Oichi, the older sibling of NT-Mama, and the uncle of 95-tan. He speaks in the manner of an old man and is respected for his wisdom and experience. After the OS Wars, he retired and occasionally tests his skills with other OS-kuns like 2K-kun and XP-kun.

Appearance

He wears a Meiji-era military uniform in green, occasionally blue or lilac like his sister 3.1-tan. His attire includes a "3.1" medal, and he carries himself with the air of a seasoned veteran.

Notes

  • Known for his serious and stoic personality.
  • Often depicted enjoying traditional Japanese pastimes.
  • Rarely appears in modern OS-tan depictions but is respected for his role in the OS-tan Wars.
