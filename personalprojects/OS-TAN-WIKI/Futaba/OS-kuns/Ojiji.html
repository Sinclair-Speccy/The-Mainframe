Description

Ōjiji is an OS-kun that personifies Windows 3.1 to 3.11. Based upon various artworks and posts from Futaba, he maintains a serious demeanor and enjoys traditional Japanese activities such as tea and rice crackers. Unlike his sister Ōbaba, he is accompanied by a DOS cat girl rather than DOS Kitty. Ōjiji is portrayed as the elder brother of Ōbaba and Oichi, the older sibling of NT-Mama, and the uncle of 95-tan. He speaks in the manner of an old man and is respected for his wisdom and experience. After the OS Wars, he retired and occasionally tests his skills with other OS-kuns like 2K-kun and XP-kun.

Despite being retired and having a serious demeanor some artworks show him wearing a disco attire and having an afro, suggesting a more casual side to his character.

Appearance

Based upon various artworks and posts from Futaba, he wears a Meiji-era military uniform in either purple (to match his sister, Oobaba) or green. His attire includes a "3.1" medal, and he carries himself with the air of a seasoned veteran.

Notes and Trivia

  • While he is seen with a green attire other artworks depict him in a purple outfit. This may have been done to align him with 3.1-tan more but it depends on the artist's choice.
  • Rarely appears in modern OS-tan depictions.
