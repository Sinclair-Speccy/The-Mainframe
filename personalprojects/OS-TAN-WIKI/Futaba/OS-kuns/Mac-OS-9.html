Description

Kurou is the personification of Mac OS 9 and the younger brother of Sonata, and the older brother to the OSX-kuns. He is a gentle and introverted character who often feels inadequate compared to his more powerful sisters. Kurou has feelings for Me-tan and is sometimes depicted with her. He tends to work behind the scenes but takes on a more prominent role in interactions with Me-tan.

Appearance

Kurou is designed as a silver-haired boy wearing a bodysuit with sleeves reminiscent of Sonata's design.

Notes and Trivia

  • Kurou can draw like Sonata, but not very well.
  • His Names is a play on words, with "Kurou" meaning "ninth son" and also being a homophone for "hardships" or "troubles" in Japanese.
