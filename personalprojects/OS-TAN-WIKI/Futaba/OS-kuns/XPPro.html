Description

XP-kun represents Windows XP Professional Edition. He is portrayed as a muscular man in his late teens or early 20s, with long black hair tied in a low ponytail with a red bow, and blue or dark grey eyes. Often depicted shirtless, wearing a collar, cuffs, and a long loincloth, with "XP"-shaped scars on his chest.

Appearance

XP-kun is a tall, muscular young man with a kind and cheerful demeanor. Despite his brash and sometimes impulsive nature, he is gentle and respectful, especially towards animals and children. He often spends time with 2K-kun and Me-kun, who watch over his training. He also looks out for his younger brother Homeo, sharing a mutual understanding about dealing with their sisters.

According to the Trouble Windows OS-tan Fan Book, anthropomorphized OS characters are not exclusively female. In February 2004, male versions of the anthropomorphized OS characters was introduced. Initially, it was treated as a female type of the same, but as the character became established, it was recognized as a brother and referred to as "OS son".

In addition to being called "OSたん", "OSタン", etc., it also started to be called "OS daughter" to pair with the sons. By eventually gaining siblings. The anthropomorphised OS characters acquired more human-like qualities and variations in their daily lives. This allowed them to break away from the trend that had been biased towards the "weak two-sided" aspect centered on female characters.

Furthermore, the existence of the son has played a role in gaining female supporters for the anthropomorphized OS characters, such as original developments unique to the Futaba Yaoi board.
