Description

The Tora Brothers are OS-kuns personifying various versions of Mac OS X, ranging from Cheetah to Tiger. They are the younger brothers of Sonata and Kurou and are known for their macho appearance and rigorous training under Kapone. They hold Sonata in high regard and willingly take care of household chores for her. The Tora Brothers are mischievous, often depicted as athletic and having a weakness for women.

Appearance

The Tora Brothers are depicted as macho men with animal traits, eventually transforming into beast-headed cats. They use specific honorifics and speech patterns, referring to themselves with "washi" and ending sentences with "~ja." They often speak collectively, delivering punchlines together.

Notes and Trivia

  • They refer to the Windows Family as "Madoshu" and have a sense of rivalry with them.
  • They are known for their unique five-part punchline conversation.
  • Despite their minimal interactions with others, they cause sweltering heat around them.
