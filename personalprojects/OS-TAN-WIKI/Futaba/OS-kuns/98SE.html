Description

Windows 98SE-kun is the OS-kun personifying Windows 98 SE, and the younger brother of 95-tan, 95-kun, 98-tan, and 98-kun, and the elder brother of Me-tan and Me-kun. Known for his alter-ego Masked Vulcan SE, he actively participated in the OS Wars and was presumed dead after going missing in action. After the wars, it was discovered that he was alive and running a coffee shop near the Windows family home.

Appearance

98SE-kun is depicted as a young man in his mid-20s with long ice-blue hair and light blue eyes. He often wears turtlenecks and basic pants, sometimes tying his hair into a ponytail. He has a more feminine appearance and demeanor compared to his brother 98-kun and often wears "SE" earrings. In his Masked Vulcan SE alter-ego, he dons navy blue armor with reversed colors from his sisters' outfits.

Notes and Trivia

  • Known for his alter-ego Masked Vulcan SE and participation in the OS Wars.
  • Presumed dead but later found running a coffee shop.
  • More feminine in appearance and demeanor, enjoys having fun, and is a capable fighter.

  • Assuming the date for when the OS Son vault was made which was July 9, 2004 is correct, he would have appeared around this time as the last update was on May 30, 2005 and exists in the side menu list under 98 like his brother.
