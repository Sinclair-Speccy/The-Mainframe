Description

Dr. Norton is a program-kun personifying Norton Antivirus, known from the Futaba Channel. He often appears alongside Me-tan, 2K-tan, and XP-tan.

Appearance

Dr. Norton is depicted as an old male doctor with a white lab coat, a yellow tie, glasses, a stethoscope, and the old Symantec logo on his forehead. He has an ectoplasmic tail instead of legs, referencing Norton Ghost.

According to the Trouble Windows OS-tan Fan Book on August 24, 2003, the first male character, "Dr. Norton," was introduced. This expanded the "OS Humanization" concept, which had previously included only female characters.

Notes

  • Dr. Norton is characterized as an eccentric and lecherous old doctor.
  • In "Troubled Windows," he frequently requests check-ups from the OS-tans, driven by curiosity and perversion.
  • He is known to exhibit inappropriate behavior and interests towards the OS-tans.
