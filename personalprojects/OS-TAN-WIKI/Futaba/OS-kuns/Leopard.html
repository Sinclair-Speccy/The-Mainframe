Description

Reobo is the OS-kun personifying Mac OS X Leopard. He is a mischievous boy adored by everyone in the Mac OS family. Reobo is the younger brother of Mac OS 9-tan, Mac OS 9-kun, and the OSX Cats. He uses the personal pronoun "sessha" and ends his sentences with "~de gozaru." Reobo refers to his elder brothers with "anija."

Appearance

Reobo is a young boy with blonde hair, blueish eyes, and cat traits such as ears and a tail. He usually wears a loincloth of an animal pattern, a cape or scarf, and an apple-shaped eyepatch. He is often seen in the company of Sonata-tan or other OSX-kuns and sometimes with Homeo, Kyourou, or ME-kun.

Notes

  • Reobo is a childhood friend of Visbou, and they often pull pranks on the Windows Family.
  • He takes care of his younger sister Yukihyo in various ways.
