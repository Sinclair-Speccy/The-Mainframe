Description

Homeo personifies Windows XP Home Edition. He is the youngest of the XP siblings and is characterized as a timid, hesitant boy often disturbed by his eccentric sister, Homeko. Homeo is often teased into being a model for Homeko's yaoi doujins, often forced to work as an assistant on manuscripts and dressing up in female clothes.

Appearance

Homeo is a short bot, has long green hair, usually tied in a ponytail, and green eyes with glasses. He often wears a key-shaped hairclip and is usually seen in shorts and a coat with long sleeves. He also enjoys cosplaying as female characters.

Notes and Trivia

  • Often teased by his sister Homeko.
  • Attended Me-gaku (maid academy) dressed as a woman and befriended some of the Nijiura Maids.
  • Has a crush on Yukiko but is afraid his true identity will be revealed.
  • Gets along with 98-tan but is often afraid of her.
  • Frequently helps his sister with her doujinshi activities.
