Description

Windows 98-kun is the OS-kun personifying Windows 98, and the elder brother of 98-tan, 98SE-tan, Me-tan, and Me-kun. He is also the younger brother of 95-tan and 95-kun, with the Windows NT lineage, such as 2K-tan, being his half-siblings. Known for his serious yet laid-back nature, he took on the alter-ego Masked Vulcan during the OS Wars, fighting actively. After the wars, he worked as a pro wrestler but is now unemployed, living on an allowance from his grandmother.

Appearance

98-kun is depicted as a young man in his mid-20s with black or brown hair, usually wearing casual clothing like a t-shirt or a long sleeve black shirt with plain pants. He sometimes sports "98" earrings or a "98" tattoo on his left shoulder. In his Masked Vulcan alter-ego, he wears mech-like armor inspired by Kamen Rider, with a "98" logo and a long scarf.

Notes

  • Known for his alter-ego Masked Vulcan and participation in the OS Wars.
  • Unemployed and often seen at arcades and pachislots.
  • Cares deeply for his siblings, maintaining a calm demeanor and looking after them.
  • Occasionally referred to as "Uncle" by his younger sisters despite being their brother.
