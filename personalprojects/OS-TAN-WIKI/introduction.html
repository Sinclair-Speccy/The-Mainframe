Introduction

This small wiki is by me, Sinclair-Speccy, to compile my own personal research on OS-tans and to share with people who care.

How to navigate?

To use this site, you can use the tabs at the top to go to your desired location. Information on the OS-tans is under "Settings" with the option to pick "Futaba Channel" or "+Nijiura+" which will have their respective entries.

Some entries may have character's debut date in the infobox. Please note that some of the debut dates listed are based purely on the earliest known appearance that I could find.

You may notice that some pages have a wiki called "Futaba★Secondary Backstage (2jiwiki)" listed for other links. For reasons this wiki does not have direct links to entries over there due to the Futaba★Secondary Backstage wiki asking for people to not link back to it.
