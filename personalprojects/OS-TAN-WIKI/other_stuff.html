Wikis

Before using these wikis it should be important to note that no matter what wiki you use, none will ever be 100 right about info on OS-tans. Why? While they may use sources even those sources address that the articles are not the official settings, nor are they valid just because they were written. This applies to all the wikis that are known.

We will most likely never see the original thread where ME-tan was shown for the first time, therefore we will never really know what was intended of her and whenever the info about her on other wikis is 100 correct. This same can be applied to most of the other characters, if not all of them. The wikis are still valuable, but people shouldn't assume they're 100% accurate as sources and the Futaba characters can be compared to characters from other series where a lot is left to interpretation such as Touhuu or Vocaloid.

  • Futaba★Secondary Backstage - A wiki dedicated to summarising the characters and knowledge of Futaba Channel. Can be found usually if you search "2jiwiki" as the wiki doesn't want people linking back to it.
  • OS-tans.moe - A wiki recommended only for more up-to-date information on the Futaba and Madobe OS-tans.
  • OS-Tan Collections - Another wiki recommended only for reading up on fan characters and the gallery.
