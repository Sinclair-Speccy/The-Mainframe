As an overview, Time Goes By was made in 2017, inspired by a game called Plazma Burst 2. The story focues around a boy named Quiv who was known as a "Earthlander" as he stayed behind on Earth when everyone fled to Mars. One day he accidently activates a old spaceship that was claimed to no longer be in use, only to have the ship fly off in space. he gets stuck with another person who had that spaceship as their home.

The main focus of the story was about what could happen in the future if we didn't look after the planet but well, seems like some of the stuff from the story is already happening...

On the right side are naviagtion buttons to go to each section. This page only serves as a page to take you to other sites for now. Might add lore and all later?
