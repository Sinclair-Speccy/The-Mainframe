Short Biography

Yuriko Larson, born on 11 APR 2011, is a teenager who began her high school journey in 2023. She is a resident of Meadows Ridge and has lived there all her life. Yuriko is best known for her distinctive long brown hair, striking green eyes, and pale skin. Standing at a height of 5'5", she often dresses in average attire, but what truly stands out is her Dearest Dream necklace with a precious emerald, as well as her trusty Vindictive shortsword.

Yuriko's journey took an unexpected turn when she met the "Guardians of the School." After experiencing a chilling nightmare, she encountered the guardians the following day. Alongside her best friend Serina McIntosh, Yuriko became intertwined with their mission to safeguard the school from malevolent forces... or are they malevolent forces?

History and Background

Yuriko Larson was born on 11 APR 2011 in Meadows Ridge, where she has spent her entire life. In 2023, she embarked on her high school journey at Meadows Ridge High School, marking the beginning of a new chapter in her life.

During her high school years, Yuriko formed a deep and enduring friendship with Serina McIntosh, a bond that would prove essential in the challenges that lay ahead.

Yuriko's life took an unexpected turn when she experienced a haunting nightmare, a prelude to the extraordinary events that would soon unfold. The following day, she encountered the enigmatic "Guardians of the School," a group dedicated to protecting the school from otherworldly threats.

Initially dismissive of the guardians' claims that she was the chosen one, Yuriko believed she was trapped in an ongoing nightmare, refusing to accept the surreal reality confronting her.

As time passed, a series of peculiar incidents began to plague Meadows Ridge High School. Books inexplicably disappeared from the library shelves, and other inexplicable phenomena occurred, raising suspicions and unsettling the community.

Yuriko's path intersected with that of Karasu, the elusive figure responsible for the mysterious occurrences. Encountering him during his raid on the school's cooking classroom, she embarked on a mission to uncover the truth behind the incidents and put an end to them.

Yuriko's resolve and persuasive skills led her to convince Karasu to abandon his disruptive activities, marking a turning point in their relationship.

As her journey continued, Yuriko delved deeper into the realms of the supernatural. The Guardians of the School persisted in their efforts to convince her of her pivotal role, granting her a glimpse of her own potential and gifting her the Dearest Dream necklace. This enchanted necklace, adorned with an emerald, bestowed upon her the ability to access the enigmatic "Ozzer Vorld," an alternate dimension where ghosts and the supernatural coexist.

Further revelations awaited Yuriko as she crossed paths with the enigmatic Government of Agglomeration. This encounter shed light on a looming threat posed by a defector intent on sowing chaos within their world.

Yuriko's destiny intertwined with those of her friends and allies as they united to confront the defector's sinister intentions. Faced with the weight of responsibility, Yuriko and her companions embarked on a perilous journey to thwart the defector's plans and preserve the delicate balance between worlds.
