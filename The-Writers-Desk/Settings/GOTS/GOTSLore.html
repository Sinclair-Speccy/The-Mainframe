Guardians of the School Lore

Lore time! Here, you'll find intriguing background stories and details of the world.

    Discover the ancient order of guardians entrusted with protecting the balance between realms. Learn about their origins, training, and the secrets they safeguard from the realms' inhabitants.

  • The Ozzer Vorld

    Explore the mysterious supernatural realm that intertwines with the everyday world. Uncover its unique inhabitants, landscapes, and the hidden portals that connect it to the human realm.

    Unveil the significance of magical artifacts scattered across both realms. The Dearest Dream necklace, a potent symbol of destiny, plays a pivotal role in shaping the fate of characters and the realms.
