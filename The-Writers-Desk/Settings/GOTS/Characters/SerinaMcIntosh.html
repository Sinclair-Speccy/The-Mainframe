    Serina McIntosh

    Character Info

    • Full name: Serina McIntosh
    • Gender: Female
    • Height: 5'5"
    • Eye colour: Blue
    • Hair colour: Blonde
    • Species: Human
    • Faction: Government of Agglomeration

    ~~~~~~~~

    Appearance

    Serina McIntosh possesses a captivating and ethereal appearance. Standing at a height of 5'5", she carries herself with grace and poise. Her flowing blonde hair complements her piercing blue eyes, which seem to hold a depth of wisdom and insight. Serina's physical presence exudes a sense of tranquility, reflecting her inner strength.

    Personality

    Serina's personality is a harmonious blend of wisdom and empathy. Her words are carefully chosen, and her precise speech pattern reflects her thoughtful nature. Empathetic and compassionate, she is a source of guidance and support for those around her. Serina's unwavering belief in the power of inner strength inspires those who cross her path.

    Backstory

    Serina McIntosh's early life is veiled in mystery, with the details of her birth year remaining unknown. Her journey intertwines with Yuriko Larson's as they formed an unbreakable bond during their high school years at Meadows Ridge High School. Yuriko's endearing nickname "Sara" adds a touch of warmth to their friendship, a name bestowed upon her by Yuriko herself.

    Serina's role as a supportive presence comes to the forefront when she is sent to aid Yuriko and Karasu in their battle against a nefarious defector. With her ability to transform into an albino raven, Serina provides essential assistance and unwavering guidance.

    Relationships

    Serina's relationships are characterized by her unwavering support and empathy. Her close bond with Yuriko Larson is evident, as they navigate challenges together with shared determination. Serina's role as a confidante and pillar of strength extends to her interactions with Yuriko and Karasu, offering both practical guidance and emotional solace.

    Abilities

    Serina McIntosh's remarkable abilities extend beyond the mundane, as she possesses the extraordinary power to transform into an albino raven. This unique skill empowers her to traverse realms and contribute to the ongoing battle against negativity.

    Quotes

    "The present is a gift, a fleeting treasure that unfolds before us. Embrace it, for it is a chance we may never encounter again." - Serina McIntosh
