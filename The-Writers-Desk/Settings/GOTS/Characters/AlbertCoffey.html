Appearance

Albert Coffey, known by the nickname Karasu, possesses a haunting yet captivating appearance. With an imposing height of 5'5", he carries an air of mystery that lingers around him. His dark brown eyes reflect an enigmatic depth, while his distinctive feature includes hauntingly dark circles beneath his eyes. His natural black hair cascades around him, adding an element of otherworldly charm.

Personality

Karasu's personality is a tapestry of complexity and intrigue. Educated in his speech and mannerisms, he often displays an air of quiet contemplation. His strengths lie in his ability to navigate shadows and unearth hidden truths. However, his weaknesses include a reluctance to embrace his past and a palpable sense of detachment from the world. Motivated by a desire for redemption and a longing for peace, Karasu's goals remain shrouded in secrecy.

Backstory

Born in the city of Meadows Ridge on the 15th of February 1898, Albert Coffey's journey was one of both tragedy and resilience. His high school years began in 1901 at Meadows Ridge High School, where he encountered challenges both academically and socially. Despite his efforts to join the school's drama club as a means of overcoming his shyness, his experience was marked by isolation and struggles.

An unfortunate series of events cast a dark shadow over Albert's life. In the wake of a mysterious murder during the school's annual athletics event, Albert found himself entangled in a web of suspicion. Despite his innocence, the weight of circumstantial evidence and societal isolation led to his wrongful conviction, culminating in a fate that defied his intentions.

Relationships

Karasu's connections are both profound and scarce. While unable to fully relate to his peers due to the temporal divide between their eras, he finds solace in his alliances with Jevana and Yuriko. They serve as his pillars of practical advice and emotional support, bridging the gap between his haunting past and uncertain present.

Abilities

Karasu's enigmatic nature extends to his unique abilities. One of his remarkable talents is the power to transform into a raven, a symbol that reflects his name's origin, meaning "raven" in Japanese. This transformative skill enables him to navigate realms beyond the ordinary and unlock hidden truths.

Quotes

"The court of truth knows no closing hour; its judgment echoes through eternity." - Albert Coffey

Trivia

- Albert Coffey's moniker, "Karasu," is derived from the Japanese word for "raven," a name that encapsulates his mystique and transformative abilities.
