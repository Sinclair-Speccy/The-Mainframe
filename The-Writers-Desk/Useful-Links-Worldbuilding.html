Useful Links for Worldbuilding

This section contains links to sites I find useful when it comes to worldbuilding.

  • Supernatural Powers and Abilities: A Wikia wiki that has a list of supernatural powers and abilities.
  • Family Echo: A simple online website for making family trees. Can be saved in storage unless cookies and history are cleared. Trees can be exported as Gedcom files.
  • English to Shakespearean: An exaggerated English to Shakespearean translator.
  • Bryn Donovan's writing inspiration and resources: A blog with all sorts of writing inspiration and resources.
  • Dictionary of Interjections: A list of English interjections.
  • Online Symbolism Dictionary: A symbolism dictionary.
  • Character Chart for Fiction Writers: Useful for developing fictional characters.
  • The Internet Movie Script Database (IMSDb): Access movie scripts for dialogue and research.
  • Colorpedia: Color encyclopedia with human names for all colors.
  • 638 Primary Personality Traits: A comprehensive list of personality traits.

Tools and Generators

Links to tools and generators I use for worldbuilding:

  • Azgaar's Fantasy Map Generator: Interactive and highly customizable maps.
  • Fantasy name generators: Generates names for fantasy characters.
  • Days between 2 dates: Calculator for figuring out days between two dates.
  • How Old Was I on a Certain Day?: Calculate your age on a specific date.
