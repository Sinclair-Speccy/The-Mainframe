In hindsight, no, I was not looking forward to it. I had no actual Apple computer running macOS or a classic Mac anywhere in my house to even partake for MARCHintosh.

What is MARCHintosh?

As an extract from the official MARCHintosh site:

With the success of such events as DOScember and SEPTandy, why shouldn't the Macintosh retrocomputing community have their own month to share their passion for classic Macintosh computers? MARCHintosh has been an exciting online event since its creation in 2021. We're excited to continue this tradition in 2023!

And that's where you come in...

We're asking that content creators from all communities and platforms - even folks who don't normally produce Macintosh retrocomputing content - work together to help spread the word about #MARCHintosh - a month-long event starting on Wednesday, March 1st, 2023.

I had no plans to partake in MARCHintosh at all until on the 7th of March I remembered the Twiggy Mac prototype existed and could be emulated...

The Twiggy Mac and Sony Test pre-release version of System 1
